Co-Working

MT Cali offers freelancers, entrepreneurs, and small businesses professional space and opportunities to develop projects with other professionals.

Our Space

MT Cali shared office space provides friendly and professional environment to local and traveling professionals with a high speed (50+ Megs) and reliable internet connection.

Services

MT Cali offers IT Consulting, VoIP (free calls around the world), Cloud Storage, and High Speed Laser Printing. All network services ride on a true end-to-end Gigabit network, one of the fastest networks available to the public in Cali.

Events

MT Cali's coworking community provides networking opportunities, and facilitating collaboration between independent professionals, entrepreneurs, and small businesses. We consistently host local IT meetup events.
