Co-Working

Co-working space for early startups.

Coworking allows freelancers, entrepreneurs, and small businesses of different sectors to share the same workspace physically and/or virtually to develop their professional projects independently or jointly with other professionals.

We offer our members the following amenities:

  • Reliable, High-Speed Internet with download speeds of 60+ Megs

  • Local Gigabit Wired Network and Wi-Fi Network (Certified G/N/AC)

  • High capacity laser printer, local fax, scanner, copier on our local network

  • Professional environment to conduct meetings with clients

  • Secretary chair, monitor, keyboard, and mouse at each station

  • Telephone service covering 60 countries for free for our members

  • 24/7 Facility Access

  • Personal laptop/tablet required

Technology and Business Mentoring

In addition we offer our invaluable expert technology and business consulting for freelancers, entrepreneurs and small businesses to help them grow and expand.

Pricing for individuals

  • Monthly Membership: $350.000 COP due by 5th of the month

  • Weekly Memberships: $100.000 COP due up front for that week (a week constitutes 5 days)

  • Daily Memberships for Collaborators: $20.000 COP up front

  • Daily Membership: $30.000 COP up front

  • Company/Corporate Membership: This is negotiated with owner

Included Services:
Monthly Members:
  • 24 Hour access to facility (pending required security brief)

  • 10 Printed Pages for free (does not rollover per month) from laser printer

  • Access to proxy internet with 60 Megs of Download

  • Wi-Fi Access for Guests

  • Access to shared space

Weekly Members (5 Business Days M-F):
  • Open from 8am-10pm (will have door code)

  • Printing on demand (pricing per page)

  • Access to proxy internet with 60 Megs of Download

  • Wi-Fi Access for Guests

  • Access to shared space

Daily Members (5 Business Days M-F):
  • Open from 8am-10pm (will Not have door code)

  • Printing on demand (pricing per page)

  • Access to proxy internet with 60 Megs of Download

  • Wi-Fi Access for Guests

  • Access to shared space

Collaborators vs. Daily Members:

Daily members are stand-alone individuals who wish to use MTCALI services. Collaborators are those specifically invited by Weekly/Monthly. Members to work with them. Weekly/Monthly Members are fully responsible for collaborators, collaborators at no time should be in MTCALI without their sponsor.

IT Services

Integrity and Excellence in all we do.

At Morrison Technologies, we focus on technology solutions that help entrepreneurial individuals and small businesses achieve results quickly and effectively. There is no one solution that will cover all aspects of day-to-day management of information technology. However, with the right set of resources and proper focus, anyone can manage technology and demystify it to an everyday tool that is there to help you grow your business and your bottom line.

Entrepreneur/Sole Business Owner

Web Presence: This includes website development, outsourcing, management, search engine optimization (SEO), Google/Yahoo/Bing web service integration, and social media integration.

Software Configuration Management: We provide bootstrapped entrepreneurs and businesses the best software available, regardless of your needs, to outfit your laptop/desktop to maximize results and leverage the latest software packages.

Consulting: Whether you are new or have experience in technology, our ability to show you how to improve your processes and organize a solid plan to tackle some of the toughest information technology hurdles are our specialty. Avoid re-inventing the wheel and let us share our experience with you and your business.

Laptop/Mobile Device: Notebooks/laptops, netbooks, and smartphones allow us to operate nearly anywhere at any time. Such mobility, however, brings its own set of challenges such as synching across platforms (phone, laptops, desktops) as well as data security , integrity, and confidentiality.

Cloud Computing: The storage of information in a central place in order to have it anywhere at any time has been a long held promise of technology that is finally coming to mass market fruition. However, as this technology matures, we must choose the best solutions that fit our needs while ensuring the security, availability, integrity, and confidentiality of our data.

Non-Profits and Local Government

All Services Mentioned Under Entrepreneur / Sole Business Owner and SMB PLUS...

Pricing and Payment Terms: We take pride in helping non-profits and small local government streamline their IT needs in order to focus on their main mission of serving others. We provide flexible payment plans and reduced pricing on most services.

Small and Medium Businesses (SMB)

All Services Mentioned Under Entrepreneur / Sole Business Owner PLUS...

Security: Data theft, identity theft, and electronic financial theft are just a few of the security issues that have skyrocketed over the last decade. Securing networks and assets (desktops, laptops, smartphones) against both internal and external threats is no longer an option in today's marketplace where a single data incident can bankrupt a business. We show you the best industry practices and procedures to best secure your assets and information.

Organizational Controls and Security: Disaster recovery, security policies, inventory control, acceptable use policies all share a common thread, the lack of any in the majority of businesses that operate in today's data driven environment. Too many businesses realize the critical nature of disaster recovery and business continuity planning only after a catastrophic event occurs. While we wish every business the best, let us help you prepare for the worst case scenario before you ever run into one

Education and Training: The best computing systems and software will do very little for any business or individual who lacks the skill or ability to run such systems or software. We provide online training courses on various topics to help our customers use their resources to their full potential.
